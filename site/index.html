背景：安第斯病毒 在人际间独特传播,且致死率高,因而引起对暴露后预防的关注。 主要方向：本文回顾利巴韦林的抗病毒原理、基于模型推导的潜在给药方案以及有限的临床数据。 研究现状：利巴韦林在体外及动物模型中对 汉坦病毒 显示有效,但针对 ANDV 暴露后预防的人体证据极其稀缺,且毒性是一个关注点。 不足：尚无对照的人体试验,最佳剂量和疗程尚不明确,安全性顾虑使其仅限于特定高危暴露人群使用。 后续研究：开展对照临床研究以明确利巴韦林暴露后预防的疗效、安全性和剂量方案,并结合药代动力学模型及对 ANDV 暴露的系统监测。

Ribavirin post-exposure prophylaxis for Andes virus exposure: a viewpoint.

Background: Andes virus uniquely transmits between humans and has a high case fatality, prompting interest in post‑exposure prophylaxis. Main directions: the viewpoint reviews ribavirin’s antiviral rationale, potential dosing regimens derived from modelling, and the limited clinical data. Current state: ribavirin shows in‑vitro and animal efficacy against hantaviruses, but human evidence for ANDV PEP is extremely scarce and toxicity is a concern. Gaps: there are no controlled human trials, optimal dose and duration are uncertain, and safety concerns limit use to selected high‑risk exposures. Future research: controlled clinical studies to define efficacy, safety, and dosing of ribavirin PEP, supported by pharmacokinetic modelling and systematic surveillance of ANDV exposures.
